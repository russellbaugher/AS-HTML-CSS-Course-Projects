Thai Dishes

This is only a short list of my personal favorite local dishes, there many so many delicious items to chose from! These dishes may vary by region.

Pad Thai

Famous dish of thin rice noodles stir-fried with egg, tofu and shrimp. Seasoned with fish sauce, sugar, tamarind, vinegar, and decorated with bean sprouts and ground peanuts.

Tom Yum

Spicy herb-filled broth is often referred to in English-language menus as 'sour Thai soup'. Shrimp is the most popular variety of this dish. An oily chili paste gives it the spicy punch it’s known for. Many herbs and lime juice contribute to its flavor.

Green Curry

This spicy dish has coconut as a base and a curry paste made of green peppers. Simmered with basil, bamboo shoots, coriander, and eggplant. Most popularly served with chicken and over jasmine rice.

Morning Glory

Morning glory, a common leafy green in Southeast Asia, prepared stir-fried with garlic, chilies, oyster sauce, fish sauce, and fermented black been. Crispy and refreshing green option.

Cashew Chicken

Thai stir fry chicken with cashew nuts with mixed vegetables. Honey, soy sauce and chilli are cooked with carrot, onions, mushrooms, chicken, and roasted cashews. Commonly served over rice.

Papaya Salad

Salad combining crunchy green beans, cherry tomatoes, papaya, fish sauce, and lime with a sweet, spicy, salty, and sour paste of ground chilli and garlic. This recipe may vary by region

Basil Chicken with Rice and Fried Egg

This is one of Thailand’s principal dishes that everyone enjoys. I think it is truly delicious, satisfying, and easy to make at home. The chicken can also be replaced with tofu, pork, beef, seafood, whatever your preference is.

Pad See Ew

This is a dish of fat rice noodles cooked with eggs, chicken, and bok choy. The noodles are prepared with soy sauce.

Grilled Pork Skewers

Just like it says, yummy grilled pork cooked over coals that can be found sold up and down the streets by street vendors at a very low price!

Thai Omelette

Sticky Rice and Mango

A dish to satisfy your sweet tooth mad of rice cooked with sweet coconut milk and served with sliced mangos.

Banana Pancakes

Sweet and delicious street food option. A thin dough is spread out and then a sweet sauce of egg and bananas is wrapped inside and then fried. These are then topped with a vanilla sauce with options for chocolate, strawberry or other flavors.
