Fruits of Thailand

There is an amazing variety in both flavors and colors in the fruits of Thailand!

Mango

Slightly different from the varieties available in the West. Strong, sweet flavor.

Mangosteen

Interesting appearance with thick soft shell, revealing five sweet lobes of fruit.

Rambutan

Milder sweet fruit with a one of a kind appearance.

Durian

The notorious fruit of Thailand. Most people say you either love it or you hate it. One thing is for sure, most hotels won't allow it inside because of its divisive odor.

Pineapple

A sweeter variety than those found in the West.

Dragonfruit

Bright, attractive fruit with white flesh. After pulling away the peel, there is no pit and the entire fruit can be eaten easily.

Guava

The Thai name for this fruit is also the name given by locals to foreigners.

Coconut

Nature's electrolyte drink, only lacking sodium. Used for many local dishes.

Banana

Smaller, sweet varieties than those found in the West.

Chili Peppers

In my opinion, these fiery little guys are the most popular thing grown in Thailand. The food can get pretty spicy here!

BE AWARE! Though there is normally no problem, be sure to inspect any fruit purchased at night markets and street markets for bruises, odor, holes, or pests. I have found an entire colony of ants inside of a piece of fruit I cut open at home!
